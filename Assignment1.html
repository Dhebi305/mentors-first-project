BRIEF DOCUMENTARY ON CLOWNFISH

They are carnivorous omnivores. They feed primarily on zooplankton but also eat bottom dwelling invertebrates, and algae. They also nibble on the tentacles of their host anemones at times.

Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
Average Length: 11 Centimeters
Average Lifespan: 7 years
Habitat: Tropical Coral Rects

Clownfish are protandrous hermaphrodites, meaning that they all mature as males but have the ability to change their sex to female.
